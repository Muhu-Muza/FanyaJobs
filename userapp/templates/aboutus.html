About Us

Home / About Us

We are a Company whose aim is to create a platform where
Job seekers and Employers can connect as the search
for jobs and Employees continues.


We obssess over creating a virtual environment that allows company recruiters to be able to post available job vacancies within their institutions
and allow job seekers to search for these jobs and apply for them. We've done this be honing in on unique blends for interfacing
and an intuitive display that are especially effective and truly feel incredible to use.

OUR STORY


We are a group of 5 friends, Ivan, Fairuz, Bannet, Keneth and Anold. We started FanyaJobs as a Job Portal in 2022 while studying at Makerere University Business School as our Final school project. We shared a common feeling about searching for Jobs, and wanted to lay special focus and effort to digitalise the Job search and application process, Having tried the rudimentary methods of Job search and being left unsatisfied with how things were done. Frustrated, but motivated, we embarked on the journey to research about Job search and developed our Job Portal to help solve the challenges we faced.
